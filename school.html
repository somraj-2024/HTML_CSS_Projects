About Us

DAV Public School is committed to providing quality education to shape young minds into responsible citizens. Our curriculum is designed to foster creativity, critical thinking, and holistic development in a positive learning environment.

  • Science, Mathematics, and Computer Science
  • Language and Literature
  • Physical Education and Sports
  • Art, Music, and Drama
